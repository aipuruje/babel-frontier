The Core Loop: "Invisible Learning"

Pauline Cullen's pedagogy disguised as high-stakes gameplay mechanics.

Listening: "The Wiretap"

Players intercept "enemy communications" (IELTS Listening audio). They must extract codes (answers) to prevent virtual attacks.

Drill: Note Completion, Map Labelling

Reading: "Decryption"

Ancient archives contain clues. Players speed-read to find 'keys' (keywords) before the archive self-destructs (timer).

Drill: Skimming, Scanning, T/F/NG

Writing: "The Journalist"

Player is a reporter covering a crisis. Must file reports (Task 1) and Editorials (Task 2) that satisfy the AI Editor.

Drill: Coherence, Lexical Resource

Speaking: "Negotiation"

Voice-controlled boss battles. Argue your way out of a hostage situation or convince a diplomat. Real-time AI analysis.

Drill: Fluency, Pronunciation
